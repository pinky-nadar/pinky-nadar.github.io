Contract Management App

Our app simplifies contract management by creating a centralized repository for contracts and using OCR to extract obligations and clauses.


Problem

Organizations struggle with managing contracts efficiently due to reliance on paper-based methods. This manual process often leads to inefficiencies, such as difficulty in locating specific contracts, time-consuming search processes, and challenges in tracking contract status and obligations.

The transition to a digital contract management solution is necessary to eliminate these inefficiencies and improve overall contract management processes.

Goal

Create a user-friendly digital contract management system that streamlines storage, improves search functionality, and provides clear visibility into contract status and obligations, ultimately enhancing productivity and efficiency.

Core Features

  • Contract Filtering: Efficient search with keywords and filters.
  • OCR Processing: Extract text from documents for easier search.
  • Version Control: Manage and track contract revisions.
  • Digital Signatures: Electronic signing within the platform.
  • Automated Reminders: Alerts for deadlines and renewals.
  • Dashboard: Centralized view of contract metrics and actions.

User Persona




Key Takeaways

User needs were a centralized solution to streamline contract management, reduce manual processes, and ensure compliance. Key needs include easy search, efficient tracking, and collaboration for faster contract review and revision management.


User Flow

Developed a seamless user flow for the contract management app, focusing on simplifying core tasks like contract upload, search, tracking, and digital signatures.

By incorporating features such as OCR processing, a clear contract status system, and a centralized dashboard, the designer ensured users could easily navigate and manage contracts.

The flow was designed to improve efficiency, minimize time spent on tasks, and provide a user-friendly experience.






Designs


Centralized View

The Dashboard offers a centralized view of key contract obligations, including statuses, deadlines, and pending actions.

It provides real-time updates and quick access to tasks like signing or reviewing, helping users manage contracts efficiently and stay organized.



Signatures & Collaboration

The Digital Signatures feature enables users to sign contracts electronically within the platform, streamlining the approval process.

It allows for easy collaboration by enabling multiple stakeholders to review, approve, and sign documents in a secure, efficient manner, eliminating the need for physical signatures or external tools.

This enhances workflow efficiency and speeds up contract finalization.
